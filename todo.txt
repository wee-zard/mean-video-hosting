## Implementations

### 1. Not Found page
- There is a /not-found page where the user will be nagivated when the requested url, video, or chanel is not found on the server.


### 2. Video page
- When clicking on a video, then the view count under the video will be automatically incremented by 1.
- On the right side of the page, 5 random video will be displayed where each of the videos are different then the one the user selected to watch.
- Displaying the video uploader's profile picture and the name. When the users clicks on the profile picture of the uploader, then the users will be redirected to the chanel page of the uploader.
- Under the uploaded video, the view count and the uploading time is displayed.
- About the comments under the videoo
   - The logged in users has the folowing features:
      - Users can write new comments.
      - Users can reply to comments.
      - Users can delete their own comments.
      - Users can edit their own comments.
   - Admin and the video uploader has the folowing specific features:
      - Delete any comments under the uploaded video.
      - Can hide comments of other users. The comments are hidden for other users, but visible for the comment's writer an the andmin and uploader
